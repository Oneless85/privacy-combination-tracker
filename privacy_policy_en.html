Contact: ricardop.apk.s@gmail.com

2) Data we process

  • Device identifiers & signals: Android Advertising ID (AAID)*, OS/App version, system language/region, basic performance data from Google SDKs (so ads and billing work).
  • Purchase info: Google Play Billing purchase tokens/receipts to unlock features (e.g., remove ads). We do not receive card numbers.
  • Consent: ad consent state via Google UMP (where required in EEA/UK).
  • Camera (when using the scanner): used solely to read ticket bar/QR codes. On‑device processing; no images are stored or uploaded to our servers.
  • Local preferences: language, country/game, favourite combinations — stored on your device only.

*You can reset or delete the AAID in Android settings.
