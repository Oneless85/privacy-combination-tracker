This Privacy Policy explains how Combination Tracker (“App”) processes your data. Operated by Developer.

At a glance
We collect the Advertising ID (AAID), usage and diagnostics (if Analytics/Crashlytics is enabled), and purchase data to manage “Remove ads”/Pro. We do not collect your name, email, or precise location.

1. Data we may collect

  • Advertising ID (AAID) for ads, frequency capping and fraud prevention (Google AdMob).
  • Usage (events, interactions) and diagnostics (crashes, ANR) if Analytics/Crashlytics is enabled.
  • Purchase data (token, SKU) to validate/restore purchases via Google Play.

2. Purposes & legal bases

  • Provide app features (results, alerts, checker).
  • Show ads (personalized or not, depending on consent).
  • Analyze performance & reliability; fix issues.
  • Manage purchases and remove ads where applicable.

3. Consent (EU/UK)

We use UMP to collect/manage your consent. Review choices under Settings ▸ Privacy ▸ Manage consent. If you refuse personalization, we show non‑personalized ads.

4. Sharing

  • Google AdMob (advertising & fraud prevention)
  • Google Play Billing (purchase management)
  • Firebase/Crashlytics (crash & performance logs, if enabled)

5. Retention & transfers

We keep data only as needed. Processing may occur outside your country (e.g., by Google) with appropriate safeguards.

6. Children

The App is not directed to children.
